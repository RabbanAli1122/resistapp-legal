RESIST — Delete Account

Developed by CoreForge · hello@resistapp.co

You can delete your RESIST account and all associated data at any time. There are two ways to do this.

Option 1 — Delete from within the app (instant)

1
Open the RESIST app on your device.
2
Tap the Settings icon on the home screen.
3
Scroll down and tap Delete Account.
4
Confirm the deletion. Your account and all associated community data will be permanently deleted immediately.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you no longer have access to the app, email us at hello@resistapp.co with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include your War Room username. We will process your request within 7 business days.

What data is deleted

  • Your War Room username and profile
  • All posts and comments you have made in the War Room
  • Your Firebase authentication record
  • Your upvote and interaction history

What data is not deleted

  • Personal recovery data (streak, XP, relapse log, goals) — this data lives only on your device and is never transmitted to us. To delete it, simply uninstall the app.
  • Subscription records — managed by Google Play. To cancel your subscription, visit your Google Play subscription settings.

Note on on-device data

All personal recovery data in RESIST is stored exclusively on your device and never transmitted to CoreForge or any server. To permanently erase this data, uninstall the app from your device.
