RESIST — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026
Developed by CoreForge

1. Overview

RESIST is a personal recovery tool designed to help individuals overcome pornography addiction. We have built this app with a privacy-first approach. The vast majority of your personal data never leaves your device.

2. Data Stored On Your Device Only

The following data is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. We have zero access to this data. It is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

  • Your streak start date and streak history
  • Your relapse log and timestamps
  • Your XP points and level progress
  • Your reasons for quitting
  • Your personal goals
  • Your gender identity selection
  • Your vulnerable hour preference
  • Your notification settings
  • Your AI coaching chat summaries

Deleting the app permanently erases all of this data. There is no cloud backup. We cannot recover it for you.

3. Data Collected By Third-Party Services

We use a small number of trusted third-party services to power specific features. Each service operates under its own privacy policy and data practices.

3.1 Firebase Authentication (Google)

Used for: War Room community login
What is collected: Email address OR an anonymous account identifier, depending on which sign-in method you choose
Who stores it: Google Firebase
When collected: Only if you choose to create a War Room account
You can delete this: Use the "Delete Account" option inside the app

3.2 Firebase Firestore (Google)

Used for: War Room community posts and interactions
What is collected: Your posts, username, upvote activity, and comment history
Who stores it: Google Firebase
When collected: Only if you post or interact in the War Room
You can delete this: Use the "Delete Account" option inside the app, which removes all your posts and data

3.3 OpenAI

Used for: AI Drill Sergeant feature in the Panic Support screen
What is collected: The message you send and a system prompt are transmitted to OpenAI's API to generate a response
Who stores it: OpenAI (subject to their data retention policies)
When collected: Only when you actively use the AI support feature
Important: We do not log, store, or review these conversations on our end. They are sent directly to OpenAI and the response is returned to your device.

3.4 RevenueCat

Used for: Managing your subscription and verifying purchase status
What is collected: Your subscription status, purchase history, and a device identifier
Who stores it: RevenueCat and Google Play
What we see: Only whether your subscription is active or not
Payment details: We never see or store your payment information. All billing is handled by Google Play.

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

We explicitly do not collect:

  • Your real name
  • Your phone number
  • Your physical address
  • Your location
  • Your contacts
  • Your device photos or media
  • Your browsing history
  • Any biometric data
  • Any advertising identifiers
  • Your streak, XP, or personal recovery data (this stays on your device)

We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with advertisers. We do not use your data to build profiles for marketing purposes.

5. Permissions We Request

NOTIFICATIONS — Required to send you daily check-in reminders and your vulnerable hour alert. You set the timing during onboarding. You can disable this at any time in the app settings or your device notification settings.

INTERNET — Required to connect to Firebase for the War Room community feature, to process subscription verification via RevenueCat, and to send messages to the AI Drill Sergeant. The app functions for all non-community features without an active internet connection.

We do not request access to your camera, microphone, location, contacts, or storage.

6. War Room Community

The War Room is an entirely optional feature. You can use every other feature in RESIST without ever creating a community account.

If you choose to use the War Room:

  • Your username and posts are visible to other users
  • You post under a username of your choice — your real identity is not required or collected
  • Anonymous accounts are supported — no email required
  • Posts reported 3 or more times by other users are automatically removed
  • You may delete your account and all associated content at any time from within the app settings

7. Children

RESIST is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has created an account, contact us at hello@resistapp.co and we will delete it promptly.

8. Data Security

On-device data is protected by your device's own security mechanisms. Community data is stored on Google Firebase, which implements industry-standard security practices. We do not operate our own servers that store personal user data.

9. Data Retention

On-device data: Retained until you delete the app or clear app data.
War Room account data: Retained until you delete your account from within the app.
OpenAI interactions: Subject to OpenAI's own data retention policies. We retain nothing on our end.
Subscription data: Managed by RevenueCat and Google Play per their respective policies.

10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (on-device data is always accessible to you directly)
  • Delete your data (uninstall the app for on-device data; use Delete Account for community data)
  • Request information about what data we hold about you
  • Withdraw consent for community participation at any time by deleting your account

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@resistapp.co
